Dinner For Schmucks

What We Do

Have you ever found yourself wanting to have dinner with your favorite character from a movie or show? Welcome to Dinner For Schmucks. Here we provide you with the information needed for a splendid dinner. We prepare you with a recipe and recommended alcoholic beverage to make any night special. Character quotes can be mind-bending pieces of literature that can inspire you, so we make sure to tag one of those along side your meal. Home cooking is a great way to relieve stress and have fun at the same time. Connecting with these new meals will change your life.

How It Works

Our website provides you a random recipe, alcoholic beverage, and quote from a character you select. Get ready for the best meal of your life!
